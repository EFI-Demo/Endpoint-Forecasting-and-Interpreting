
		Trending Security News This week at the RSA U.S. 2012 Conference in San Francisco, IT security executives, government leaders and security researchers and hackers gathered together for keynotes and sessions covering a range of topics impacting the industry today. Security experts shared the latest trends and issues facing corporate organizations, governments, and citizens on the Internet and discussed big data and analytics; the growth of cloud computing and expanding use of mobile technologies and consumerization of IT; and the changing threat landscape. In other news, CNET reports on Microsoft’s Windows 8 beta, which drew a million downloads in one day.
